1 ½ ounces* Midori
1 ½ ounces Cointreau or Triple Sec
1 ounce fresh lemon juice
For the garnish: 1 small dry ice cube**, cocktail cherry

can add 1/2 inch to 1 inch dry ice cube to make it bubble and boil.
